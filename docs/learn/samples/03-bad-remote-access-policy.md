> **Sample data only.** This policy is intentionally flawed for teaching purposes. Do not use as a template.

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs remote access to SABESB Treatment Plant 4 systems. It applies to all employees, contractors, and vendors.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all systems including operational technology.

## 3. Authentication

3.1 All remote access requires a username and password.

3.2 Multi-factor authentication is required for VPN access to the corporate network.

3.3 Once authenticated to the corporate network, users may access OT systems through the engineering workstation using the standard RDP client.

## 4. Vendor access

4.1 Vendors may request remote access via the IT helpdesk.

4.2 Approved vendors are granted a corporate VPN account with standard user privileges.

4.3 Vendors are required to sign a non-disclosure agreement.

4.4 Vendor sessions are not recorded due to privacy considerations.

## 5. Session management

5.1 All remote sessions automatically time out after 8 hours of inactivity.

5.2 Users are responsible for logging out at the end of each session.

## 6. Tooling

6.1 The following tools are approved for remote access: corporate VPN client, RDP, TeamViewer (for vendor support only), AnyDesk (legacy use, to be retired).

## 7. Compliance

7.1 This policy aligns with industry best practice and applicable regulations.

7.2 Violations should be reported to the IT Manager.

## 8. Approval

Approved by Plant Director, January 2026.
